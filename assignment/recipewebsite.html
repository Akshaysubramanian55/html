Delicious Chocolate Chip Cookies

Preparation Time: 30 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter,softend
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. preheat oven to 350 degrees F(175 degrees)
  2. In a large bowl,cream together the butter and sugar until smooth
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time,then stir in the vanilla
  4. In a separate bowl,combine the flour and baking soda;graduallynadd to the butter mixture
  5. stir in the chocolate chips
  6. Drop by rounded tablespoons onto ungreased cookie sheets
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until edges are golden brown
